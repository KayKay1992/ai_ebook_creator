1. Overall Purpose
This is the main hero section (the big banner at the top of your homepage). Its goal is to immediately grab attention, communicate the value of your AI Book Creator tool, and encourage users to sign up or get started.
2. Layout Structure
The hero uses a two-column responsive layout:

Left side: Text content (headline, description, buttons, stats)
Right side: Visual showcase (mockup image + two floating cards)

On mobile, these stack vertically; on large screens, they sit side by side.
3. Left Side Breakdown

Small Badge: A stylish pill-shaped tag with a Sparkles icon saying "AI-POWERED BOOK PUBLISHING". This helps set the premium tone right away.
Main Headline: Large, bold text with a beautiful gradient on "Digital Books" to make it pop.
Subtitle: A descriptive paragraph explaining the benefit — turning ideas into books quickly.
Call-to-Action Buttons:
Primary button ("Get Started For Free") — gradient and eye-catching.
Secondary button ("Watch Demo") — more subtle outline style.

Trust Stats: Three small statistics at the bottom showing social proof (50k+ books, 4.9 rating, fast creation time).

4. Right Side Breakdown
This is the visual part that makes the hero feel alive:

A large image of your dashboard (the hero.png).
Two floating glassmorphic cards that hover over the image:
Processing Card (top right): Shows the AI is actively working. Uses a pulsing animation and a Zap icon.
Completed Card (bottom left): Shows a finished book with page count. Uses emerald/green accents to signal success.

Both cards have glassmorphism (frosted glass effect with backdrop blur) and subtle hover animations.

5. Design & Animation Highlights

Modern violet/purple gradient theme that matches your branding.
Soft background gradients and glow effects for depth.
Smooth hover animations on buttons and floating cards.
Glassmorphism on the mini cards gives a premium, high-tech feel.
Responsive design that works well on all screen sizes.

6. User Experience Flow
The hero is designed to:

Catch attention with big visuals and headline.
Explain the product benefit quickly.
Build trust with stats.
Drive action with clear buttons.
Show the product in action through the right-side mockup.

This creates an emotional and visual connection — users can imagine themselves creating beautiful books using your tool.
